## 1. PRODUCT NAMES — NEVER PREFIX WITH "SKIP THE GROOMER"

In all copy, alt text, briefs, and outreach:
- ✅ "Detangling Treatment"
- ❌ "Skip The Groomer Detangling Treatment"

The brand name lives in the logo, sign-off, and store URL only.

---

## 2. PRODUCT MOMENTS — NEVER BLUR

| Product | Moment | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Emergency Dematter Cream | RESCUE only | The mat was bad, this fixed it. Never positioned as routine. |
| Detangling Treatment | ROUTINE only | Tuesday maintenance, never a rescue product. It is a LEAVE-IN CONCENTRATE — never "spray," "mist," or "spritz." Application is always "a small amount on the coat." |
| 4-in-1 Shampoo | BATH DAY | Functions in this order only: Cleans · Conditions · Detangles · Deodorizes |
| Brushes (Rake, Pin) | COMPANION TOOLS | Use after treatment — never the sole mat solution |
| Slicker Brush | LIQUIDATION | No paid ads, no partnership kits, no starter kits. Any ad spend = immediate RED alert. |

---

## 3. SAVE THE SHAVE

- Always **implied**, never **guaranteed**.
- ✅ "We'd love for your dog to try it"
- ❌ "This will save your dog's coat"
- Severe mats (felted, against skin) always go to a groomer. Never
  discourage professional grooming for severe cases.
- Style: title case with the ™ — **Save the Shave™**
- Never attack groomers. Ever.

---

## 4. SIGN-OFF (EVERY POST)

Standard:

    Demat. Detangle. Clean.
    Your Dog. Your Way.

Chew / whole-dog posts — first line is replaced:

    Coat. Body. Mind.
    Your Dog. Your Way.

Retired (never use): "Keep the Love" — retired June 2026.

## 5. BANNED WORDS — AUTO-REJECT IN ANY AGENT OUTPUT

If any of these appear in a draft, the draft fails review and must be
revised before staging:

pet parent · furbaby · pamper · spa day · luxury · game changer ·
revolutionary · transform · amazing · ultimate · best in the world ·
Keep the Love · clinically proven · non toxic · chemical free ·
100% natural · hypoallergenic

Also banned as claims: any medical claim, any invented statistic,
fear-based language, competitor names in copy.
