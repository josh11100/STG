Pillar: FUN / FAMILY / PRODUCT

## URGENT vs regular — when to use each

| | Regular | URGENT |
|---|---|---|
| Prefix | `Story:` | `Story: URGENT` |
| When | Any time — picked up at 9 AM next morning | Same-day — Josh fires manually after you post |
| Use for | Planned content, campaign briefs, calendar posts | Launch announcements, reactive content, time-sensitive |

## What Story needs to do its job

The more specific you are, the better the draft. Always include:

**Platform** — Story writes differently for Reels vs Facebook vs TikTok.
Without this it has to guess.

**Narrator** — Will is fun, confessional, dad jokes. Kathy is warm,
poetic, truth-teller. Pick one per post. Without this Story defaults
to Kathy.

**Pillar** — FUN / FAMILY / PRODUCT. This determines the tone and
whether it's a brand-building post or a conversion post.

**Brief** — One or two sentences on what the content should accomplish.
What's the moment? What's the emotion? What should the viewer feel or do?

## How to approve a draft

Story posts the draft as a **thread reply** on your original message.
Read it, and if it's good:

> Reply in that same thread with: **approved**

That's all. One word. Story picks it up on the next run and logs it
to `#stg-decisions-log` as cleared for scheduling.

If you want changes, reply in the thread with your notes and Story
will revise on the next run.

## What Story can do

✅ Facebook posts
✅ YouTube descriptions
✅ Video scripts (no voiceover / title card format)
✅ Social calendar entries (what to post, when, what asset to pull)
✅ Email subject lines + body copy (when Wren requests it)
✅ SMS copy (when Wren requests it)

---

## What Story cannot do

❌ Post directly to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or YouTube
❌ Schedule anything
❌ Write paid ad copy (that goes through Blaze + Will)
❌ Invent product claims or stats — real numbers only
❌ Use Kathy's voice on a Will post or vice versa
❌ Blur product moments (Dematter = rescue · Detangling = routine)

## Quick rules that never break

The full, always-current rule set lives in one place:
[`docs/BRAND_RULES.md`](https://github.com/josh11100/STG/blob/main/docs/BRAND_RULES.md)
— Story reads it automatically before every run, so what's in that
file is what gets enforced. The highlights:

- Detangling Treatment is a **leave-in concentrate** — never spray,
  mist, or spritz. Application is always "a small amount on the coat."
- Emergency Dematter = **rescue**. Detangling Treatment = **routine**.
- Save the Shave: **imply** the goal, never **guarantee** the outcome.
  Severe mats always go to a groomer.
- Every post ends with the sign-off:
  **Demat. Detangle. Clean. / Your Dog. Your Way.**
  (Chew posts: **Coat. Body. Mind.** replaces the first line.)
- Full banned-words list is in BRAND_RULES.md — includes "Keep the
  Love" (retired June 2026), "pet parent," "furbaby," "pamper,"
  "spa day," "game changer," "amazing," and more.

If a rule changes, it changes in BRAND_RULES.md — not here.
